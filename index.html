Future hoover car

This is future one person hoover car. It has 2 wheels at the rear of the car and the front is floating. This model has 2 1024x1024 textures, both has a normal map and a reflection map. The polycount of this model is 1522 faces, and there is also a low poly model with a polycount of 940 faces The render is made in Blender, but I made it also possible to drive the car in Unity.

Character

This is Kull a voodoo game character.
With this project I challenged myself to create
an awesome game character with at little polygons as possible,
but still keeping the smooth shading of the toon shader.
The final count is 440 polys and the level of detail is still quite pleasing.

City

This is a render of a city.
It is generated on a particle system.
The city is made of only 6 simple low poly buildings.
This is a very easy and good way to create a complete city in less than a day.
This is also made in blender 2.76.
